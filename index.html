HOMEMAKERS

About Us
The Homemakers Forum is a unique initiative by FuelBee to integrate young mothers into easy and convenient socially productive activities. Usually these young mothers who take time off from work to care for their newborn infant will need a couple of years before they can continue in their full-fledged professional career. While a gap in their work history might normally send up a red flag in their resume, they can typically spin their experience as a growth and learning opportunity through FuelBee Homemakers Forum.

These meet-ups are free of cost and hosted once a month by FuelBee. Interesting and informative topics such as Parenting, Child Development and Family Management are discussed among members, which benefits the young mothers greatly. Packed with fun and informative activities, these sessions are aimed to connect like minded women who wants to contribute and impact child development and making our neighbourhood better space for our children to grow. Come Be a Part of this Social Initiative!

Key Objectives

  • Build a strong & credible neighbourhood network
  • Give homemakers a platform for self & communal growth
  • Empower them through various awareness programs for self & social development.

Community Programs

We acknowledge that a society's well-being has its roots in the homemaker of every house. We aim to bring together such pillars of the community & neighbourhood to give them a platform to learn, explore, express & demonstrate their interests, abilities & skills for individual & communal growth.
